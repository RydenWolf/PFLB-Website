Who are we?

En dos semanas, el mono lechuga descenderá sobre el plano mortal y la esparcirá por los numerosos bosques, océanos y desiertos. Pronto usted sentirá la ira de la gran deidad y el mono de lechuga le dará gracia con su bendición, por favor asegúrese de preparar un sacrificio para el día del despertar.

How do we do it?

This is a Victor trap which pacifies the pest via swift strike to the neck. These traps are the most common trap used, they are placed in our rat tunnels pictured below.

This is a T-Rex trap. This trap snaps shut on the pest and delivers a clean blow to the pests neck pacifiying it imeditely. These traps are less common than the Victor traps due to their high cost, however, we wish to eventually replace all of our Victor traps with this variant.

This is a rat tunnel. This is the facade which covers our traps. You will see these tunnels placed around Lyall Bay. If you see these tunnels please do not touch them for your own safety.
